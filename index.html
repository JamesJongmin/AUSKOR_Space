Launch Costs 💰 PLUMMETING

95% Cost Reduction: $20K → <$1K per kg to LEO
Reusable launch technology driving dramatic cost decline: ~$20,000/kg (early 2000s) → <$3,000/kg on Falcon 9 → <$1,000/kg projected on Starship. Falcon 9 single booster flown 20+ times.
